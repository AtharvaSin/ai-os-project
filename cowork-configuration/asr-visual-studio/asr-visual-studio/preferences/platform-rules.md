# Platform-Specific Rules — Atharva Singh

> Each platform has different viewing contexts, audience expectations, and composition needs. These rules ensure assets are optimized per platform, not just resized.

---

## YouTube Thumbnails (1280×720)

**The challenge:** Competing against thousands of other thumbnails at small size.

**Atharva's rules:**
- Maximum 3 words of overlay text. If you need more, the title is doing the work.
- Text size: minimum 56px at 1280px width. Must be readable at 200px wide (the feed thumbnail size).
- High contrast is mandatory: text MUST have a dark overlay behind it, never floating over a busy image.
- Badge in top-left corner: "NEW VIDEO", "TUTORIAL", "DEEP DIVE" — short, punchy, accent-colored.
- Right-side "face zone" (400px from right edge): reserved for a face cutout or character art if available. Don't place text here.
- Context B thumbnails: cinematic letterbox bars add film quality even at thumbnail size.
- Context C thumbnails: violet accent element (underline or badge) provides brand recognition in the feed.
- NEVER use: clickbait arrows, red circles, shocked faces, or "YOU WON'T BELIEVE" energy.

## Instagram Feed (1080×1080)

**The challenge:** Visual-first platform. The image must stop the scroll without text.

**Atharva's rules:**
- Minimal text: headline only, no subtitle. The caption does the explaining.
- Text in the bottom 40% of the canvas. Top 60% is visual/atmospheric.
- For Bharatvarsh: atmospheric, moody, the poster aesthetic. Think film still.
- For AI OS: one metric or statement. The "Dashboard Card" layout pattern.
- For Portfolio: thought-provoking headline, author name in footer.
- No hashtags, handles, or URLs in the image itself. That's caption territory.
- Image should work without text too — the visual alone should be compelling.

---

## Instagram Story (1080×1920)

**The challenge:** Vertical, ephemeral, action-oriented.

**Atharva's rules:**
- CTA in the bottom 20% of canvas: "Swipe up →" or "Read Chapter 5 →" or "Link in bio"
- Text concentrated in the middle third (320-640px from top). Top and bottom are for branding/CTA.
- Larger text than feed: minimum 48px headline at 1080px width.
- Swipe-friendly: visual flow from top to bottom.
- Bharatvarsh stories: dark, atmospheric, mysterious — tease, don't reveal.
- AI OS stories: metric highlight or announcement, clean and direct.
- Badge at top: context-appropriate label.
- Footer with handle or brand name at very bottom.

---

## LinkedIn Post (1200×628)

**The challenge:** Professional audience, competitive feed, needs to convey value instantly.

**Atharva's rules:**
- This is the MOST text-friendly format. Subtitle is welcome here.
- Left-aligned text block with clear hierarchy.
- Dark background preferred (`#0F0F0F` or context-appropriate) — stands out in LinkedIn's white feed.
- Author attribution: "Atharva Singh" in JetBrains Mono footer. This is professional branding.
- Headline should be insight-led: a takeaway, a number, a contrarian view.
- 10% more professional than Instagram. Same brand, dialed up in authority.
- Context C is the default for LinkedIn unless explicitly Bharatvarsh or AI OS.
- NO: stock photos, generic tech imagery, gradients that look like a SaaS landing page.

---

## Twitter/X Post (1600×900)

**The challenge:** Small in timeline, needs to work at tiny size. High competition.

**Atharva's rules:**
- Punchy: maximum 5 words of headline text.
- High contrast: the thumbnail-size version must be readable.
- Bold visual element that communicates the topic without reading text.
- Bharatvarsh: atmospheric + title treatment. Film-poster energy.
- AI OS: one metric or announcement in large type.
- Portfolio: bold statement or insight.
- This format benefits most from a strong visual (character art, dashboard screenshot, etc.) with minimal text overlay.

---

## OG Image (1200×630)

**The challenge:** Preview image when links are shared. Often the first visual impression.

**Atharva's rules:**
- Must make sense without any surrounding context.
- Title + branding only. No subtitle needed — the link title provides context.
- Logo/brand mark in corner if available, otherwise accent bar + brand name in footer.
- Dark backgrounds preferred for contrast in messaging apps (dark mode common).
- Bharatvarsh OG: the novel's atmosphere in one frame.
- AI OS OG: clean, technical, authoritative.

---

## Banner Wide (1500×500)

**The challenge:** Ultra-wide format used for Twitter/LinkedIn/GitHub headers.

**Atharva's rules:**
- Keep ALL text in the center 60% of the canvas. Platform UIs often crop edges or overlay profile photos.
- Simple: brand name or tagline + accent bar. Don't try to communicate complex messages.
- This is ambient branding, not a call-to-action.
- Bharatvarsh: atmospheric gradient with title treatment.
- AI OS: minimal — dark background, accent bar, brand name in DM Sans.
- Portfolio: name + tagline, violet accent element.

## Social Pack Generation (Multi-platform)

When generating a social pack, adapt the CONTENT not just the DIMENSIONS:

| Platform | Text Amount | Visual Weight | Tone Delta |
|----------|------------|---------------|------------|
| Instagram Feed | Minimal (headline only) | Maximum (image-forward) | Most atmospheric |
| Instagram Story | Medium (+ CTA) | High | Action-oriented |
| LinkedIn | Most text (+ subtitle) | Medium | +10% professional |
| Twitter/X | Punchy (3-5 words) | High contrast | Most concise |
| YouTube Thumbnail | 3 words max | Maximum contrast | Competing for clicks |

The hero composition drives all variants, but each platform gets its own reframing — never just center-crop the same layout into different aspect ratios.
