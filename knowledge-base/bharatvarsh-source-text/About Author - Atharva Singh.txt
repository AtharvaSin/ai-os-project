About the Author (Long – ~160–200 words)

Atharva Singh is a GenAI and cloud product leader focused on building intelligent, adoption-ready systems that bridge advanced technology with real-world impact. 
His career spans execution and strategy: he began in planning and project controls at Larsen & Toubro Construction, moved into digital transformation consulting at Accenture Strategy & Consulting, and currently leads GenAI & cloud initiatives at People Tech Group—designing and shipping expert-in-the-loop AI platforms, vision-AI workflows, and cloud-native decision-support products. 
Atharva earned a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from NIT Durgapur and an MBA (Supply Chain & Operations) from IIM Mumbai, bringing a strong systems-thinking lens to how he designs products, processes, and operating models. 

Beyond work, he is a visual storyteller, digital artist, and long-form creator—using composition, pacing, and narrative craft to make complex ideas feel clear and compelling. 
His debut novel, MahaBharatvarsh, is a 350+ page speculative political thriller set in an alternate, high-tech Bharatvarsh—where power, governance, and freedom collide under the pressure of modern warfare, AI, and biotechnology.
