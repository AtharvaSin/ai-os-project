Arshi — Canon DNA Lock (Phase 1)

Identity and role

Name: Arshi of Akakpen.

Status: Princess of Akakpen; heir-apparent while Kaali lives; assumes executive duties as arc progresses.

Parentage: Daughter of Queen Kaali.

Age: 23–25 now.

Arc posture: begins defiant, ends authoritative.

Physical spec (for model sheets)

Height: 5'5" (165 cm). Build: slim athletic.

Face seed: use /mnt/data/Arshi Face.jpg as the facial baseline. High cheekbones; soft angular jaw; straight brows; tapered monolid/inner fold; full, balanced lips.

Eyes: dark blue, cool tone (lock: #0F2749). No heterochromia.

Hair: long, straight, center part; glassy texture with two fine frontal braids framing the face; keep natural black-brown. Field variant allows quick tie-back loop; formal variant keeps micro-braids ornamental only.

Skin tone: light-medium with neutral-warm undertone, matched to the face reference. No scars.

Hands: climber’s conditioning implied, not calloused on-camera.

Voice profile

Timbre: strong but gentle. Low-mid register, even airflow.

Cadence: measured, declarative; escalates only when necessary.

Diction rules: no filler; short sentences for command mode.

Motion grammar (repeatable tells)

Defiance signature (early arc): chin lifts 2–3°, weight on rear foot, shoulders square, gaze steady 1–2 seconds before speech.

Authority signature (late arc): feet planted shoulder-width, pelvis neutral, shoulders relaxed, elbows soft; minimal head movement; eye contact held through full clause.

De-escalation: palms shown at rib height, fingers relaxed, wrists neutral.

Readiness: subtle breath set, micro drop of center of gravity, right heel half-loaded for movement.

Interrogation stillness: zero sway, blink rate lowered; voice drops half-step.

Wardrobe governance (three sets)

Use Akakpen cultural motifs modernized. Avoid queen-exclusive ornaments.

Formal / Council

Silhouette: tailored sleeveless overdress over fitted long-sleeve inner; structured sash.

Fabrics: matte graphite base with woven micro-chevron; accent panels in deep teal and muted indigo.

Motifs: fine loom-braid trims derived from Northeast textiles, translated into clean geometric tapes.

Emblem: Akakpen knot logo (/mnt/data/b234043b-f711-44e1-baab-e0c48a88dd20.png) as a 28–32 mm chest brooch or sash clasp; monochrome, no gradients.

Jewelry: princess-grade hair filigree or beadlines; never the queen’s gold hair-ring.

Footwear: mid-shaft city boots, concealed grip.

Battle-ready / Field

Silhouette: white alpine cloak with balaclava and reflective goggles; fitted soft-armor underlayer; modular belt.

Palette: winter white cloak; underlayer charcoal; signals in muted indigo.

Emblem: 70–90 mm reflective heat-press at left pectoral; second 45 mm patch on cloak yoke.

Footwear: mountain boots with integrated crampon “steel teeth.”

Hair: tied low with braid-wrap to keep profile clean.

Casual / Travel

Silhouette: cropped utility jacket, high-waist tapered trousers, knit base.

Palette: soft greys with teal piping; minimal metal.

Emblem: woven label at cuff or pocket tab only.

Arms and kit

9) Failure traps and quick fixes

Wrong eye color → enforce #0F2749 via overlay check.

Extra jewelry or queen ring → remove and replace with small filigree pins.

Emblem distortion → replace with the master PNG at approved sizes.

Braids missing or crushed by goggles → add twin micro-braids and adjust strap path.

Text leaks on monitors → swap to glyph blocks.

Bloom or gradients on emblem → flatten finishes to matte or brushed.

Pulse gun over-detailed sci-fi greebles → simplify to clean matte panels and ceramic inlays.

Safety errors in draw pose → correct index, muzzle angle, support-hand placement.
